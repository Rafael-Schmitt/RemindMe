Features

Task Management

RemindMe offers an intuitive interface for creating, organizing, and tracking tasks effortlessly. Users can quickly add tasks with titles, detailed descriptions, and due dates, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks. The app automatically categorizes tasks into "To Do" and "Completed" lists, providing a clear visual distinction between pending and finished items. With just a click, tasks can be marked as done or reverted back to "To Do" if priorities change.

Smart Sorting & Organization

Stay on top of deadlines with flexible sorting options. Tasks can be arranged by due date (ascending) or creation date, helping users prioritize effectively. Whether you're planning ahead or reviewing past tasks, the sorting feature ensures your workflow remains streamlined. The clean, card-based design displays all essential details—due dates, creation timestamps, and task descriptions—at a glance.

Edit & Update with Ease

Made a mistake or need to adjust a deadline? RemindMe’s edit functionality lets users modify task titles, descriptions, or due dates seamlessly. The built-in modal popup ensures edits are quick and distraction-free. Combined with one-click deletion, managing tasks feels fluid and responsive, reducing clutter and keeping lists up to date.

Cross-Device Accessibility

Built with a mobile-first approach, RemindMe works flawlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops. The responsive design adapts to any screen size, while the Bootstrap-powered navbar collapses into a hamburger menu on smaller devices. Users can access their tasks anytime, anywhere, with consistent performance across all platforms.

Secure & User-Friendly

The app includes login/signup options (with routes ready for backend integration), ensuring personalized task lists for each user. Frontend validations prevent empty submissions, while error handling ensures smooth operation. Future updates could expand features like reminders, categories, or shared lists—making RemindMe a scalable solution for personal and team productivity.
